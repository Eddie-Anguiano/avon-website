What is Avon's Green Goddess Facial Oil?

Green Goddess is Avon’s introduction into the CBD skincare market. This unique product is a CBD infused facial oil. It acts as a gentle moisturizer and soothes the skin upon application. It is perfect for anyone with troubled, dry, sensitive or fatigued skin. This is also for anyone who wants luxuriously moisturized radiant skin. In addition to broad spectrum CBD, this dry oil contains hemp seed oil, jojoba oil, squalane oil, sesame seed oil and Azulane oil. It is a simple product with just eight ingredients, but it is an extremely effective anti-inflammatory and it greatly soothes away any redness of the skin. Green Goddess is fragrance free and it rapidly absorbs into the skin. This facial oil is 99% pure. There is just a touch of a safe synthetic, caprylyl glycol, which is used as a preservative to prevent bacteria from forming. The hemp is sourced from here in the United States and the shelf life of Green Goddess is three years. Each bottle contains 100 mg of CBD, cleanly formulated. Green Goddess is allergy tested and 100% vegan.

What is CBD or Cannabidiol?

Cannabidiol gives the oil an anti-inflammatory property. It also decreases excess facial oil, it visibly diminishes wrinkles, skin dullness and is an excellent antioxidant. CBD containing creams also correct ruddy skin tones and any type of redness due to irritation due to its soothing properties. In this instance, cannabidiol is extracted as a powder from the leaves and flowers and mixed with oils. Because the CBD is extracted from Hemp, the oil does not contain THC, the psychoactive ingredient that gets you high in marijuana. The CBD used in Green Goddess is a broad-spectrum CBD, which means that THC is not detectable.

What is dry oil?

A dry oil is a lightweight, non-greasy, quickly absorbing oil for the skin. One to two drops of Green Goddess absorbs into the skin in about 10 seconds. It does not leave an oily residue because it does not sit on the top layers of skin cells.

What are the other ingredients in Green Goddess?

Green Goddess also contains hemp seed oil, turmeric, jojoba seed oil, squalane oil and azulane oil. Before I list the properties of the additional ingredients, I will define a couple of terms. A humectant works like a magnet to pull water out of the air and bonds with it. This increases the moisture in the skin. Emollients are used to soften and smooth the scales of the skin. This reduces rough, flaky skin.

Azulane is from the chamomile flower. It is an anti-inflammatory, an antioxidant, and an antiseptic. Blemishes and acne can be reduced and it prevents sun damage and repairs damaged cells. Azulane also soothes irritated skin.

Jojoba seed oil is a humectant and it seals in moisture with a protective layer. It’s makeup is similar to sebum, the skin’s natural oil. So, it has the ability to balance oily skin and acts to kill bacteria on the skin and is non comedogenic, (it doesn’t clog pores.)

Squalane oil, from sugarcane, acts as an emollient to boost hydration to make skin look healthy and vibrant. It can squeeze into the spaces between the skin cells and make the skin feel smoother. Squalane is an antioxidant and it boosts the formation of collagen.

Turmeric is a powerful antioxidant. It is also an adaptogen which is a special kind of plant extract that helps the skin resist the damaging effect of stressors and helps to bring the skin cells back in balance.

Hemp Seed is rich in essential fatty acids. It has the ability to moisturize the skin without clogging your pores. This rich oil reinforces and smooths the surface of the skin, increases hydration, strengthens the skin against environmental damage and delivers antioxidants for anti-aging results.

Sunflower Seed Oil is rich in vitamin E which can help protect the collagen and elastin in the skin and reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles. It’s antioxidant properties prevent the signs of premature aging.

How do I use Green Goddess in my skincare regimen?

A good skin regimen can be made up of three to four steps. You can categorize the steps into prep (cleanse,) treat, moisturize and if it is a morning regimen, you should include a product with an SPF. Green Goddess is a facial oil which is a moisturizer. It should follow any treatment products that you use. It is a light moisturizer so it may be used with an additional moisturizer if you have really dry skin. Apply it after your regular moisturizer. Four to six drops should be sufficient for your face. You can use Green Goddess in the morning and in the evening.
