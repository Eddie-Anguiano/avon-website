Veilment Natural Spa Collection Dead Sea Salt

Israeli Dead Sea Salt contains over forty critical minerals that occur naturally in the Dead Sea. The composition of Dead Sea Salt differs significantly from oceanic salt. Salt from the ocean is 85% sodium chloride while salt from the Dead Sea contains only 31% sodium chloride and the rest is made up of dried minerals and other salts. Dead Sea Salt was used by the people of Ancient Egypt and has been used in various bath products since then. High concentrations of magnesium may be helpful in improving skin hydration and in reducing inflammation. The citrus-ginger scent is truly invigorating.
