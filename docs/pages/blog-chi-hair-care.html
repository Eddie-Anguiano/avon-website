Why is CHI sulfate and PABA free?

Sulfates are used to maintain the lather in the shampoo. They grab ahold of the dirt, grime and oils. Harsh sulfates can also strip your hair of necessary natural oils which can dry your hair out and fade hair color. Sulfates can cause irritation of the scalp and the eyes as they are sulfur-containing salts. However, less irritating components can clean the hair without over stripping it or drying it out. That is why the Avon formulations do not use sulfates. PABA, or para-aminobenzoic acid can also be another ingredient that can irritate skin and cause allergic reactions. After all, your hair shaft is connected to your scalp and oil glands. It can also cause sun-sensitivity of the scalp. Avon’s CHI formulations contain no PABA.

What are the benefits of Hibiscus, Organic Aloe Vera, and Pomegranate?

Aloe Vera is a natural humectant, which means that it reduces the loss of moisture. Dry hair is prone to breakage and split ends. Because the Aloe acts as a humectant, it provides strength to the hair shaft and prevents it from splitting. Pomegranate is antioxidant rich. Antioxidants can protect hair follicles from free radicals which can damage the follicle. Healthy follicles regulate hair growth. Pomegranate also helps to provide a proper PH for the hair. A proper PH value prevents hair from becoming dry, brittle or frizzy. Hibiscus is rich in amino acids and vitamins. Since hair is made of proteins and amino acids make up proteins, amino acids are beneficial. They can make the hair stronger and increase its elasticity. Hibiscus also keeps your natural hair color vibrant. The vitamins that Hibiscus provides such as C and riboflavin, improves blood circulation and stimulates healthy hair growth.
