Introducing... The Face Shop

& my top picks!

What is The Face Shop?

The Face Shop, a Korean brand of skin care and makeup has finally arrived at Avon. Discover Avon's new collaboration with cutting edge Korean Beauty Products. The Korean beauty market is known for its formula innovations and advanced technological packaging and applicators. Whether it is skincare or makeup, all formulas are luxurious, trendy and oh so good for your skin! There is nothing like a lipstick that is the perfect shade of red or a foundation that glides on effortlessly leaving your skin looking flawless. Avon’s Face shop provides both and more! Treat yourself this holiday season with Avon’s Face Shop! Here are my top picks from the launch!

purchase

Flat Velvet Lipstick

One of the Face Shop’s newest products is the Flat Velvet Lipstick. It is a uniquely shaped flat square tip lipstick which shapes, lines, contours and fills lips effortlessly. It is a velvety matte finish infused with calendula oil which soothes, argan oil which has antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties, and jojoba and olive oil to keep winter lips smooth and moisturized. The shape of the tip keeps lipstick from feathering and bleeding. It comes in ten highly pigmented delicious shades. Four of the ten available shades are red. Those reds are, Darjeeling Red, a deep dark warm red, Cassis Red is a sumptuous cool red, London Red is a bright true red and Scarlet Red is a true warm red. The other colors available range from Solar Beige to Be My Pink. I love the unique shape of these lipsticks because they offer flexibility for lining the lip and filling the color in.

purchase

Ink Tints

A lightweight, long lasting lip stain is another new product from the Face Shop that you will see in the Avon Brochure. This vivid lip stain comes in two finishes, matte and shine. The applicator is teardrop shaped which provides for a precise, full application. The Tatoo Matte version dries to a matte stain while the Serum Shine leaves a deep tint with a high gloss finish. These Ink Tints also come in four shades of red, twelve shades in total. If you plan on using matte lipsticks, you might want to lightly exfoliate your lips once or twice a week. Matte lipsticks go on best when your lips are smooth and kept moisturized . A chemical exfoliant is great for this. Alpha hydroxy acid is the best chemical exfoliant. Avon has two products with AHA’s. One is a very mild cream, AHA Refining Cream, and there is also a stronger exfoliant, Extra Strength Retexturizing Peel. To keep your lips moist, a product such as Anew Vitamin C Antioxidant Lip Treatment is a great product. This treatment which nourishes with vitamins C and E makes lips look and feel smooth and conditioned.

purchase

Ink Lasting Primer

Before you apply your foundation, prepare your skin with Avon’s Face Shop Primer. Why should you use a foundation primer? A primer increases the life of your foundation so that you need less touch ups throughout the day. The Ink Lasting Primer turns your skin into a smooth poreless canvas that keeps your makeup in place all day. Primers also prepare the skin by giving it a smooth base that corrects imperfections by minimizing and blurring them. This colorless gel primer minimizes pores, absorbs oil and provides a matte surface that increases the staying power of your foundation. This primer also lightly hydrates your skin with rose oil and prune extract.

purchase

Toneup Primer

This primer not only lightly hydrates the skin and prepares it for foundation, it also uses color correction technology to minimize blotchiness and to correct undertones. These primers just melt into the skin and do not need much foundation to cover them. The Lavender Toneup Primer corrects sallow or yellow tones in the skin. This also helps if you wish to use cool colors for your lips, eyes and cheeks. Toneup Primer in Mint neutralizes red tones and blotches. This covers the redness from blemishes and a ruddy complexion. For pale skin, the Pink Toneup Primer adds brightness to the skin. The use of the Lumiere Toneup Primer with an iridescent pearl base adds radiance to the complexion.

purchase

Ink Lasting Foundation Slim Fit

This versatile formula is a buildable formula. It can be used for medium to full coverage. The formula is long wearing but is not cakey. It feels weightless and airy. As this foundation evens out skin tone, it also leaves a velvety matte finish. The color selection is exceptional since it comes in eighteen shades in cool, warm and neutral hues. The categories are divided up into light, warm and deep. Each category is comprised of two cool shades, two warm shades and two neutral shades. When you are applying color to your face such as eyeshadow, lipstick and blush, it is important to keep your colors in the same categories as your foundation whether they be warm (w), cool (c) and neutral (n).

purchase

Ink Lasting Cushion Foundation Slim Fit

This is the same formula and color selection as the Ink Lasting Foundation Slim Fit. However, this foundation comes in a unique compact. This technologically advanced compact is portable and delivers the perfect amount of foundation to its sponge with no mess or spillage. The compact has a mesh screen that controls the amount of foundation that is deposited onto the sponge. The sponge is made 3of an antibacterial material. Because the formula is the same as the Ink Lasting Foundation, it is also buildable. However, it does have the ability to be dispensed in smaller amounts for lighter coverage. With that being said, it is also an excellent item for a touchup if you have already applied your makeup with the Ink Lasting Foundation.
